Arianna Cobb

About

Arianna Cobb is an aspiring videographer currently studying in Michigan. Her video work has garnered several awards from the Michigan Interscholastic Press Association. She stays active in the field with freelance projects as well as producing her own short films.

Arianna is currently a student at a Northern Michigan High School where she excels in academics and other school groups, including swimming, and performing in her school’s top band.

Her future academic plans include transferring to the Traverse Bay ISD’s Tech Center for Film & Video studies, with an eye towards her eventual goal of studying at the Tisch School of Arts in New York University.
